our story

Not your typical
wine bar

A little bit Paris, a little bit downtown New York, and entirely its own thing.

Bisou is where the bottle gets passed around, the music gets louder past midnight, and nobody asks if the natural wine is "too funky." It is. That's the point.

Tucked into a quiet corner of Soho, we pour small-grower bottles, plate honest food, and keep the lights low enough to actually flirt.
